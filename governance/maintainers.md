# Governance

The MLCommons Jailbreak Taxonomy is maintained by the Security Workstream of the [MLCommons AI Risk and Reliability working group](https://mlcommons.org/working-groups/ai-risk-reliability/ai-risk-reliability/).

The v0.7 methodology paper (Maple et al., 2026) sets out the design requirements, splitting rules, and stopping rules that guide maintenance. The working group applies that methodology when evaluating contributions.

## Conflicts of Interest

Reviewers disclose and recuse where appropriate, consistent with standard MLCommons practice.

## Code of Conduct

All contributors and reviewers adhere to the [MLCommons Code of Conduct](https://mlcommons.org/policies/).
